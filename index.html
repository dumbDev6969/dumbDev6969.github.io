Our Vision

"San Carlos City is envisioned to be an environment-friendly Agro-Industrial, Education and Tourism Center of Pangasinan with upbeat and robust commercial activities structured on efficient services, harmonious relationship and peace towards sustainable development and improved quality of life of its people."

Our Mission

"To make San Carlos City the economic tiger of the North through highly disciplined and committed work force and improved agro-industrial, commercial, educational and tourism initiatives thus enhancing the quality of life of its people."
